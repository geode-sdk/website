# Introduction

Last updated: 2026-06-21

This privacy policy covers what data we (Geode developers) collect, how we use it and how it is protected. This applies to any information collected through your use of:

* This website
* Geode CLI
* Geode mod loader
* Geode launcher for Android / iOS

# What data we collect

## Anonymous telemetry

When you use the Geode mod loader, we automatically collect minor, anonymous technical data to help understand how our software is being used and fix bugs. This includes:
* Geode version
* Operating system/platform
* Geometry Dash game version

This data is entirely anonymous, and is used purely for analytical purposes.

## Developer accounts

If you are a developer and choose to sign in with a GitHub account, we collect:
* Your GitHub username
* Your GitHub user ID

This information is necessary to verify your identity and allow you to publish/manage your mods.

## Website analytics & cookies

We use **Google Analytics** on our website to track basic, aggregated statistics. Google Analytics may use cookies to gather this data. This helps us improve our website and understand our user base.

# Data sharing

We do not share or sell any data with third parties, besides the absolutely necessary data collected by Google Analytics and GitHub, for purposes described above.

# Your rights & data control

We respect your rights under the GDPR, and if you have a developer account on our website, you have the right to:
* Access the data we have about you.
* Request deletion of your developer account and associated data.

To make a request, or if you have any questions or privacy concerns, please contact us at: [admin@geode-sdk.org](mailto:admin@geode-sdk.org)

# Updates to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our software, website, or legal requirements. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
